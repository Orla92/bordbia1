Close Out Options

We strongly advise that you upload close out evidence of non-compliances directly within your portal. However, if you are unable to do so, you may also submit closeout evidence through the following ways:

  • SMS: 083 180 0069 (include herd number)
  • Email: closeout@bordbia.ie (herd number in subject)
  • Post: Bord Bia Helpdesk, Tralee Road Industrial Estate, Castleisland, Co. Kerry
